Create a Safety Plan

A safety plan can help you stay safe during a crisis. Consider including:

  • Warning signs that a crisis may be developing
  • Coping strategies that have helped in the past
  • People you can contact for support
  • Professional contacts and crisis numbers
  • Ways to make your environment safer
